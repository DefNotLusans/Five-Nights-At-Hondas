About Us

My name is Jay, and I'm 20, and my partner Eros, who is 22, and we run the shop that sells Five Nights at Hondas! One of us is stuck in the south where it isn't safe nor accepting, as well as a household that is hard mentally. The other lives across the country in a very mentally abusive and unaccepting house and they don't feel safe there either. We started our shop and the GoFundMe in order to help us get out as soon as possible. We've been together for over two years, and it's been amazing. Our situations have been getting worse, and it's becoming more apparent that we need to get out. We don't make enough to do this on our own without years of saving, and we can't stay here until that happens. We just want to feel safe and happy and have a place where we feel comfortable to be ourselves. Our three cats, one being Honda himself, are coming with us, and we hope this is able to help us get out sooner.
